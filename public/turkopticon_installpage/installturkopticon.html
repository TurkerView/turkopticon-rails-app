Know who you're working for.

Do you work on Amazon Mechanical Turk HITs? Want to avoid getting defrauded by bad requesters and help fellow Turk workers do the same?

Turkopticon lets you see any reports of bad requester behavior as you browse HITs. It was inspired by 67 MT workers' statements of rights.

Turkopticon is a Firefox extension you can use with Firefox 3 or above.
